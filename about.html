About Samarthya

Samarthya, a term imbued with significance and vibrancy, encapsulates the essence of the annual celebration hosted by the Department of Computer Science & Information Technology. Rooted in the rich tapestry of Indian culture, Samarthya conveys the notion of capability, proficiency, and empowerment.

Mark your calendars because this year, on March 23rd, we're gearing up for an electrifying extravaganza that you won't want to miss! Get ready to immerse yourself in a day filled with thrilling events, captivating competitions, and an unforgettable carnival experience.

Samarthya isn't just any ordinary celebration; it's a fusion of innovation, talent, and sheer excitement. Whether you're a coding whiz, a tech enthusiast, or simply looking for a good time, there's something for everyone at this year's event.,
Get ready to unleash your inner tech guru and let the celebration begin! See you at Samarthya '24- where the magic of technology meets the thrill of celebration.

About Department of CSIT

The Department of Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) was established in 2020 and provides undergraduate programs (B.Tech.) and doctoral programs (Doctor of Philosophy). The faculty members have extensive expertise and make significant contributions to the overall growth of the students in the department.
This four-year undergraduate program combines tool-based, skill-driven learning with engineering. While emphasizing the value of prototype construction, this curriculum also excels in preparing students for industry billing and recruitment right away. There are numerous instruments related to every course in every specialization that are important to the industry both now and in the future. This course examines the opportunities that the global IT industry is presenting. This course covers everything from the practical tools used in web development to the managerial aspects of IT.

About KL University

Established in 1980 as Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation, KL University initially began as one of the first private engineering colleges in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.

The university was named after Koneru Lakshmaiah, a renowned industrialist, educationalist and philanthropist. The university attained autonomous status in 2006 and in 2009 was named KL Deemed to be University. Although popularly known as KL University, officially the name of the university still appears as KL Deemed to be University.
Spread over a large campus of 100 acres, KL University offers a wide range of programmes ranging from fine arts to pharmacy. The university is especially renowned for its industry focused curriculum and its green campus.
